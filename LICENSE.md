## 🛠️ Troubleshooting Common Issues

**❌ Script Does Not Execute After Injection**
The executor may have failed to attach properly to the Roblox process. This is the most common issue new users face with The Lost Front Script.
**Solution:** Close both Roblox and the executor completely, reopen the executor as administrator, relaunch Roblox, wait until you are fully loaded into the game lobby, then inject and execute again.

**❌ ESP Overlays Flicker Or Disappear**
Flickering usually means the render loop is fighting with the Roblox frame pipeline. This happens more often on lower-end hardware running The Lost Front Script with all ESP options enabled.
**Solution:** Reduce `MaxRender` to `400` in `config.lua`, disable skeleton ESP, and ensure your GPU drivers are up to date.

**❌ Aimbot Locks Onto Teammates**
The `TeamCheck` flag may be set to `false` in your configuration, causing The Lost Front Script Roblox Aimbot to ignore team allegiance.
**Solution:** Open `config.lua` and set `TeamCheck = true` under the Aimbot section, then reload the config in-game.

**❌ Triggerbot Fires Too Slowly Or Not At All**
A high `Delay` value or a conflicting keybind can make the triggerbot seem unresponsive inside The Lost Front Script.
**Solution:** Lower `triggerbot_delay` to `50` ms and verify your hold key is not bound to another in-game action.

**❌ Script Crashes On Mobile**
Android executors occasionally run out of allocated memory during The Lost Front Script initialization.
**Solution:** Close all background apps, clear the executor cache from `Settings > Apps > Executor > Clear Cache`, and re-inject.
